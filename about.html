About GullyPaw

GullyPaw is a youth-led civic-tech platform that uses AI and crowdsourcing to rescue stray dogs more efficiently. With real-time tagging, location-based reporting, and NGO integration, we bridge the gap between citizens and animal welfare organizations.

Our Mission

To empower everyday citizens to become street dog saviors by making rescue as simple as a snap and tag.

Our Vision

A world where no stray dog suffers unseen. A society where compassion meets technology.
