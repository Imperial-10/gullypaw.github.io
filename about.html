About GullyPaw

GullyPaw is a youth-powered AI-based platform to rescue and track street dogs across India. We make it easy for citizens to report injured, aggressive, or lost dogs with a photo and GPS location. Partnered with CUPA, BBMP, PFA, and dozens of local shelters, we route every report to nearby NGOs and volunteers.
